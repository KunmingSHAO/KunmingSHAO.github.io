## Profile

I am a PhD candidate in Electronic and Computer Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) and the AI Chip Center for Emerging Smart Systems (ACCESS). My research focuses on AI accelerator architecture, digital compute-in-memory, ReRAM/SRAM hybrid architectures, compiler automation, edge RAG systems, and quantization-aware efficient ML deployment.

I am advised by **Prof. Chi-Ying Tsui** and **Prof. Tim Kwang-Ting Cheng**. I expect to graduate in Summer 2027 and am interested in postdoctoral opportunities related to AI hardware and efficient intelligence systems.
